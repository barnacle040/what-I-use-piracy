How to Sideload iOS Apps

Follow these steps to sideload iOS apps:

Note: You will have to connect to a pc and refresh the App and appstore using Altserver every week to continue using them .

Before Installing AltStore

Download and install the latest version of iTunes and iCloud directly from Apple (not the Microsoft Store).

  • iTunes Direct Download Links:
    • Windows 64-bit
    • Windows 32-bit
  • iCloud Direct Download Link:
    • Windows 32-bit and 64-bit

Installing AltStore

  1. Download AltServer for Windows.
  2. Extract AltInstaller.zip and run Setup.exe.
  3. Search for "AltServer" in your Windows taskbar and click "Run as administrator." Allow access to private networks if prompted. AltStore will appear as an icon in the taskbar.
  4. Connect your iOS device to your computer and make sure it is unlocked. Trust your device with your computer if needed.
  5. Open iTunes and enable "Wi-Fi sync" on your device.
  6. Click the AltServer icon in the bottom-right taskbar. Then go to “Install AltStore” and choose your iOS device.
  7. Enter your Apple ID email address and password. This will allow AltServer to authenticate with Apple and configure your account to support sideloading apps.
  8. Wait a few seconds. An AltServer notification should appear saying AltStore has been successfully installed onto your device.
  9. Restart your device if AltStore does not appear on your Home Screen.
  10. On your iOS device, go to Settings → General → Profiles & Device Management. Tap “Trust [Your Apple ID],” then tap “Trust” again to confirm.

Enable Developer Mode (iOS 16+)

To use AltStore on iOS 16 or later, you need to enable Developer Mode on your device:

  1. Go to Settings → Privacy and Security → Developer Mode.
  2. Enable Developer Mode and restart your device if prompted.

You are now ready to sideload apps using AltStore!
